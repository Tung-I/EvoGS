  L0  topk 32.242 | thr 32.298 | Lapis 32.256   (813k/816k vs 808k)
  L1  topk 30.845 | thr 30.936 | Lapis 30.806   (669k/771k vs 2.14M)

  L3  topk 26.495 | thr 26.891 | Lapis 26.971   (683k/1.13M vs 7.7M)

BIG FINDING — REVERSES the raw-garden threshold no-op: here threshold beats top-k
  by +0.09/+0.20/+0.40 dB (gain GROWS with depth), and threshold L3 (26.891)
  essentially MATCHES LapisGS (26.971, -0.08 dB) at 6.8x fewer Gaussians (1.13M vs
  7.7M); beats LapisGS at L1 (+0.13) at 2.8x fewer. Reconciliation: the lever is
  STARVED vs SATURATED, not resolution. Raw-garden top-k was ~2.5M leaves at full
  res (saturated -> threshold no-op). garden_lapis top-k is UNDER-densified: leaf
  count nearly FLAT across levels (669k->666k->683k) because 1% top-k can't keep
  up with the aggressive LapisGS pyramid; threshold grows 771k->920k->1.13M and
  recovers the L2/L3 deficit. So gradient-threshold split is the right criterion
  precisely when the budget would otherwise starve the model.

  (leaves 267->43.8 MB); even compressed, thr 26.425 > topk 26.161 (+0.26). LapisGS
